Octopus Hearts

Did you know octopuses have three hearts? Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. Interestingly, when they swim, the heart that pumps blood to the body stops beating—making swimming a bit of a workout for them!

Bananas Are Berries!?

Botanically speaking, bananas are classified as berries! Surprisingly, strawberries are not berries because they don’t meet the botanical criteria. It’s all due to how seeds are distributed and the structure of the fruit.

Sharks Existed Before Trees!

Sharks have been swimming in Earth’s oceans for over 400 million years, long before trees appeared on land! They are among the planet's most ancient survivors, predating dinosaurs.
